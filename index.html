Singular SPDEs Pattern Formation Renormalization

I am a PhD student in Mathematics at TU Munich in the TopMath Program, working in the group for Multiscale and Stochastic Dynamics under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Christian Kuehn. My research lies at the intersection of stochastic analysis, dynamical systems, partial differential equations, and mathematical physics. More specifically, I am interested in the stability of patterns arising in singular stochastic partial differential equations (SPDEs) and their interplay with renormalization.
